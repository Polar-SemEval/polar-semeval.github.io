🚀 Participation

SemEval 2026 Shared Task

We welcome researchers, students, and practitioners from around the world to participate in this task. Whether you’re a solo researcher or part of a large team, your contributions are valued!

🧩 Who Can Participate?

Anyone with an interest in NLP, sentiment analysis, social computing, or multilingual language processing is encouraged to join. You can participate as:

  • An individual
  • A research group
  • A student team
  • An industry team

No prior experience in SemEval is required.

🧪 Submissions & Evaluation

Participants will submit their model predictions via CodaLab during the evaluation window. Detailed submission guidelines and formats will be available on the Task Documentation page.

You may submit multiple runs during the evaluation phase. Final system descriptions must accompany your submission to be eligible for official ranking and presentation.

🏆 Recognition & Publication

Top-performing teams will:

  • Be featured in the official SemEval 2026 proceedings
  • Present their approach at the SemEval workshop
  • Gain visibility in the NLP research community

All teams that submit system descriptions will be included in the task overview paper.
