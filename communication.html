Communication Guidelines

  • General questions: Use Discord for quick questions and community discussions
  • Important announcements: Delivered via Google Groups
  • Technical issues: Post on Discord or contact organizers directly
  • Private concerns: Contact organizers through the organizers Google Group

Response Times

We aim to respond to queries within:

  • Discord: Within 24 hours during business days
  • Email/Google Groups: Within 48-72 hours
  • Urgent issues: We monitor all channels regularly for time-sensitive matters
