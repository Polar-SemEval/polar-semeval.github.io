👋 Welcome to the POLAR Shared Task!

Join us in advancing research on online polarization across multiple languages, cultures, and events.

🎯 The Goal

Explore and address the challenge of online polarization by analyzing a diverse multilingual and multicultural dataset spanning multiple real-world events.

📚 About POLAR

POLAR is a benchmark dataset featuring over 23k instances in seven languages, annotated for polarization presence, type, and manifestation. It supports robust and adaptable NLP research on digital polarization worldwide.
