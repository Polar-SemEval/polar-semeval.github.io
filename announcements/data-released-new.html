Key Information for Participants

Submission Guidelines

  • Final Submission: Only your last submission will be considered for official ranking
  • Platform: All submissions must be made through the CodaBench platform
  • Deadline: January 9, 2026 at 23:59 AoE (Anywhere on Earth)

What to Submit

For each subtask you're participating in, please submit:

  • Prediction files in the required format (see submission guidelines)
  • Brief system description (1-2 paragraphs) in the submission form
  • Team information and contact details

Evaluation Metrics

All subtasks will be evaluated using Macro F1 score:

  • Subtask 1: Macro F1 for binary polarization detection
  • Subtask 2: Macro F1 for multi-label polarization type classification
  • Subtask 3: Macro F1 for multi-label manifestation identification

Important Reminders

  • System description papers are mandatory for final ranking inclusion
  • Paper submission deadline: February 2026 (exact date TBD)
  • Results will be announced after the evaluation period ends
  • Questions? Join our Discord or contact organizers

Technical Support

If you encounter any technical issues during submission:

  • Check the FAQ page for common solutions
  • Post in our Discord #technical-help channel
  • Contact organizers directly via Google Group

We're monitoring all channels closely during the evaluation period and will respond promptly to any issues.

Good luck to all participants! We look forward to seeing your innovative solutions to multilingual polarization detection.
